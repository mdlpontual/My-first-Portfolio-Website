About me

Hello! I am a aspiring software developer seeking my first full-time job in the technology field. I have a degree in architecture and urban planning from the Federal University of Pernambuco, with experience in the development and management of residential and corporate projects.

These experiences allowed me to learn and enhance crucial interpersonal skills such as communication, adaptability, and organization.

I am a curious individual who enjoys learning new technologies. My joy comes from creating things and solving puzzles, and I am always looking for ways to make my work more effective and efficient.
